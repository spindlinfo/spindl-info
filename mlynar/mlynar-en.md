# 👨‍🌾 Miller's Nature Trails

Discover the history and nature of Špindlerův Mlýn. Choose one of the three loops and get a reward at the City Information Center at the end!

### 🟢 Miller's Green Loop (1.1 km)
*Short walk focused on the town's history and the origin of its name.*
* **Highlights:** "Na Skále" Viewpoint, Sun Stones, history of climate spas.
* **Difficulty:** Very easy (approx. 45 min).
* **Strollers:** ❌ **NO** (includes stairs and rocky terrain).

### 🔴 Miller's Red Loop (2.8 km)
*The classic "Miller's Circuit" through the most interesting parts of the town center.*
* **Highlights:** Historical buildings, mountain settlement history, alpine farming.
* **Difficulty:** Low (approx. 1.5 hours).
* **Strollers:** ✅ **YES** (paved roads and sidewalks).

### 🔵 Miller's Blue Loop (3.1 km)
*A nature trail along the Elbe river with beautiful views.*
* **Highlights:** Elbe river information, Krkonoše nature, settlement of the mountains.
* **Difficulty:** Moderate (approx. 2 hours).
* **Strollers:** ❌ **NO** (forest paths and uneven terrain).
